A comprehensive guide to terminology used throughout Blackhaven documentation.

## A

### APY (Annual Percentage Yield)
The real rate of return earned on an investment, accounting for the effect of compounding interest.

## B

### BAM (Backing Arbitrage Module)
Blackhaven's automated mechanism that captures value when RBT trades above or below its Net Asset Value, converting market volatility into treasury growth.

### Baseline Markets
A token market infrastructure that establishes guaranteed price floors (BLV) backed by locked liquidity.

### BLV (Baseline Value)
The lowest tradable price on-chain, set by Baseline Markets and backed by reserves.

### Bond
A fixed-term instrument where users exchange USDM for discounted RBT that vests over time.

## C

### Cooldown Period
A 7-day waiting period after exiting an HPN before the position becomes claimable.

## D

### DAT (Digital Asset Treasury)
A foundational treasury backbone that powers long-term ecosystem infrastructure.

### Debt Ratio
A metric that influences bond discount rates based on protocol conditions.

## E

### ERC-721
The Ethereum standard for non-fungible tokens (NFTs), used for HPNs and RBT Notes.

### Early Exit
Redeeming a position before its maturity date, typically incurring penalties.

## F

### Forfeit
The loss of accrued rewards and/or principal portion when exiting early from protocol instruments.

## G

### Governance
The on-chain decision-making process where HVN holders vote on protocol parameters and direction.

## H

### HPN (Haven Protected Note)
Principal-protected deposit instruments issued as ERC-721 NFTs that earn yield from treasury strategy deployment.

### HVN
The governance token of Blackhaven, giving holders voting power over protocol decisions.

## L

### Liquidity Manager
The protocol component that creates and manages Uniswap V3 LP positions from bond deposits.

### Linear Vesting
A schedule where tokens unlock proportionally over time rather than all at once.

## M

### MegaETH
The Layer 2 blockchain ecosystem where Blackhaven operates.

### Mega Points
Ecosystem rewards earned through qualifying activities on MegaETH.

## N

### NAV (Net Asset Value)
The per-token value of RBT calculated by dividing total treasury assets by circulating RBT supply.

## P

### POL (Protocol-Owned Liquidity)
Liquidity that the protocol owns permanently, rather than renting from external LPs through incentives.

### Premium
When RBT market price trades above its backing value.

### Premium Capture
The BAM's function of selling treasury RBT when price exceeds NAV, capturing the difference.

### Principal Protection
The guarantee that a user's original deposit can always be recovered, regardless of market conditions.

### Proximity Market
MegaETH's system allowing market makers and dApps to bid for sequencer-adjacent positioning.

## R

### RBT (Reserve-Backed Token)
Blackhaven's reserve-backed token whose value is tied to treasury assets.

### RBT Notes
Fixed-term lockup instruments that provide guaranteed yield on locked RBT.

### Reserve Treasury
The central vault holding all reserve assets that back RBT.

## S

### sHVN
Staked HVN, representing participation in governance and reward distribution.

### Slippage
The difference between expected and actual trade execution price.

### Strategy (Treasury Strategy)
Whitelisted DeFi protocols where treasury capital is deployed to generate yield.

## T

### Term
The duration of a bond, HPN, or RBT Note commitment.

### Treasury
The protocol's reserve holding all assets that back RBT and fund operations.

## U

### USDM
The stablecoin used for deposits and bond purchases in Blackhaven.

### Uniswap V3
The decentralized exchange protocol used for Blackhaven's liquidity positions.

## V

### Vesting
The process of tokens gradually becoming available to claim over time.

## W

### Whitelisting
The governance-approved process of adding new strategies or protocols to the approved list.
